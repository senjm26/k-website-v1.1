About

Kalyan K. Sen is an inventor, power engineer, and educator whose work focuses on improving electric transmission performance through advanced power-flow control technologies. He has served in senior engineering roles across industry and academia and is an active volunteer leader within IEEE.

Core expertise

  • Flexible AC Transmission Systems (FACTS) and transmission power-flow control
  • High‑power electronics design (concept → prototype → demonstration)
  • Teaching, technical writing, and global knowledge transfer
